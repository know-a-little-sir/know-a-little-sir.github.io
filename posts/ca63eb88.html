英语谚语

发表于2023-06-08|更新于2023-06-08|摘录
|阅读量:

1、路是走近的，经验是积累的。
The road is approaching, and experience is accumulated.
2、莫等闲，白了少年头，空悲切。
Don”t wait idle, white young head, empty sad.
3、众人里面有圣贤，土石里面有金银。
There are saints in the crowd, and gold and silver in the earth and stone.
4、打鱼的不离水边，打柴的不离山边。
Fishing does not leave the water, firewood does not leave the mountain.
5、书山有路勤为径，学海无崖苦作舟。
There is a way to learn from the sea without cliffs.
6、鞭打的快马，事找的忙人。
Whipping a fast horse is a busy man.
7、痒要自我抓，好要别人夸。
Itches need to be caught by oneself, so that others can praise them.
8、公鸡打架头对头，夫妻吵嘴不记仇。
Cocks fight head-to-head, husbands and wives quarrel without revenge.
9、胆大走遍天下，胆小寸步难行。
It”s hard to walk bravely all over the world.
10、出门看天色，炒菜看火色。
Go out to see the sky, stir-fried vegetables to see the fire.
11、人忙天不忙，迟早一路黄。
People are not busy in busy days, sooner or later all the way yellow.
12、知之为知之，不知为不知，是知也。
To know is to know, not to know is to know.
13、小人记仇，君子长志。
Villagers bear grudges, gentlemen have long aspirations.
14、人补桂圆蜜枣，田补河泥水草。
Man fills sweet jujube, field fills River mud, water and grass.
15、人在屋檐下，不得不低头。
People under the eaves have to bow their heads.
16、黑发不知勤学早，白首方悔读书迟。
Black hair does not know how to study early, white head regrets to study late.
17、常说口里顺，常做手不笨。
It is often said that a smooth mouth is not a clumsy hand.
18、不怕家里穷，只怕出懒汉。
They are not afraid of being poor at home, but of being lazy.
19、户家人的见识，田头上的本事。
Family insights, Tiantou skills.
20、刀钝石上磨，人笨人前学。
Knives and blunt stones are polished, and fools learn before they learn.
21、光说不练，枉学百年。
Speaking without practicing is a hundred years”study in vain.
22、愁人苦夜长，志士惜日短。
Sorrowful people spend long nights, but ambitious people cherish short days.
23、艺高人胆大，胆大人艺高。
Artists are bold, bold and artistic.
24、豆腐多了一泡水，空话多了无人信。
Tofu is a bubble of water, empty talk is not believed.
25、积累知识，胜过积蓄金银。
To accumulate knowledge is better than to accumulate gold and silver.
26、宁打金钟一下，不打破鼓千声。
Better strike a golden bell than break a thousand drums.
27、家有一老，犹如一宝。
An old family is like a treasure.
28、勤劳是个宝，一生离不了。
Diligence is a treasure that can never be separated from one”s life.
29、知不足者好问，耻下问者自满。
He who knows not enough asks well, but he who asks shamefully is complacent.
30、水往下流，人争上游。
Water flows downstream and people compete upstream.
31、常在有时思无时，莫到无时想有时。
Sometimes I think about it all the time, but sometimes I don”t think about it all the time.
32、舌头是肉长的，事实是铁打的。
The tongue is long and fleshy, but the fact is that it is beaten with iron.
33、儿子疼小的，媳妇疼巧的。
Son pain is small, daughter-in-law pain is delicate.
34、会嫁嫁对头，不会嫁嫁门楼。
Will marry the opposite, will not marry the gate.
35、虚心使人提高，骄傲使人落后。
Modesty enhances man, but pride makes him lag behind.
36、只要功夫深，铁杵磨成针。
As long as the work is deep, the pestle is ground into a needle.
37、修树趁早，教子趁小。
Tree-building is done as early as possible, and the Godson is done as little as possible.
38、人生病，不舒服；秧生病，不长谷。
People are sick, uncomfortable; seedlings are sick, not valley.
39、活到老学到老，学到八十仍嫌少。
It”s never too old to learn. Eighty are still too young to learn.
40、会走走不过影，会说说不过理。
You can”t walk away, you can”t talk properly.
41、从小差一岁，到老不同年。
From one year apart to different years old.
42、吃人的嘴软，论人的理短。
Eating people”s mouth is soft, but it”s short.
43、天上钩钩云，地上雨淋淋。
Sky hook clouds, rain on the ground.
44、有理摆到事上，好钢使到刃上。
Rationality is in the matter, good steel is in the edge.
45、宁给穷人一斗，不给富人一口。
Better give a fight to the poor than a mouthful to the rich.
46、吃米带点糠，老小都安康。
Eat rice with some bran, all the young and old are healthy.
47、愿为众人死，不为一人亡。
Wish to die for all, not for one.
48、积钱不如教子，闲坐不如看书。
It”s better to save money than to teach children, and to sit idly than to read books.
49、棉花云，雨快淋。
Cotton clouds, rain fast.
50、刀不磨要生锈，人不学要落后。
Knives must rust without grinding, and people should not lag behind.
51、无妻不成家，无梁不成屋。
No wife, no home, no beam, no house.
52、一年富，拾粪土；十年富，多种树。
One year is rich, pick up dung; ten years is rich, many kinds of trees.
53、背后不商量，当面无主张。
There is no discussion behind the scenes and no opinions face to face.
54、人生有限，学问无涯。
Limited life, boundless learning.
55、亲身下河知深浅，亲口尝梨知酸甜。
He who goes down the river knows his depth and tastes his pear knows his sour and sweet.
56、一勤生百巧，一懒生百病。
A diligent student makes a hundred tricks, a lazy one makes a hundred diseases.
57、树不修，长不直；人不学，没知识。
Trees do not grow straight; people do not learn, no knowledge.
58、不怕穿得迟，就怕脱得早。
Not afraid to wear late, afraid to take off early.
59、马好坏骑着看，友好坏交着看。
The horse is good or bad to see, friendly or bad to see.
60、一寸光阴一寸金，寸金难买寸光阴。
An inch of time and an inch of gold make it difficult to buy an inch of time.
61、宁做蚂蚁腿，不学麻雀嘴。
Better be an ant”s leg than a sparrow”s mouth.
62、宁死不背理，宁贫不堕志。
It is better to die than to disobey, and it is better to be poor than to be corrupt.
63、粮食补身体，书籍丰富智慧。
Food supplements the body and books enrich wisdom.
64、子不嫌母丑，狗不嫌家贫。
Dogs don”t dislike their mother”s ugliness or their family”s poverty.
65、宝剑锋从磨砺出，梅花香自苦寒来。
April showers bring May flowers.
66、谷要自长，人要自强。
Gu should grow up by himself, and man should strengthen himself.
67、迟干不如早干，蛮干不如巧干。
Better late work than early work; better brute work than skillful work.
